Closed until further notice!

Regrettably we have taken the decision to close the Hall until further notice as of the 19th of March. We have cancelled a number of events leading up until the beginning of May with a view to review at that time. Staff will be in the building catching up with other work, cleaning and generally making the place better for when we reopen!

We are happy to answer any questions regarding the history, contents and anything else and will keep in touch on Facebook, email fordgreenhall.museum@gmail.com, or call 01782 537696
